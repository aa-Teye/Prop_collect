📖 Supervisor Guide

How to use PropCollect

🎥
Tutorial Video
Video guide coming soon
1
🔐 Login

Select your name and enter your 4-digit PIN to access the system.

2
👤 Select Your Collector

Choose one of your assigned collectors and select today's date.

3
📋 Record Bill Deliveries

For each bill record the delivery outcome:
✅ YES - Bill delivered successfully: Enter ratepayer name, amount paid and payment method.
❌ NO - Bill not delivered: Select the reason for failure.

4
🔍 Search Any Bill

Use the search bar to find any bill by account number across all 11,040 bills in the system.

5
📸 Review & Submit

Click Review & Submit on each bill to confirm and save the record.

6
📊 Track Progress

The progress bar shows how many of the 160 bills have been submitted.

7
🔄 Switch Collectors

After finishing one collector go back and select another collector.

⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTE: This system is for SUPERVISORS only. Collectors do not use this app.
